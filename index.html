About us

Know us Better

Objective

Our story begins with a shared passion for renewable energy. Fueled by a vision of a world where clean power is accessible to all, we embarked on a journey to redefine the way energy is harnessed and distributed. What started as a commitment has blossomed into Dzuwagleam, a hub of solar excellence.

Vision

At Dzuwagleam, we envision a world where clean, sustainable energy powers every corner, lighting up lives and fostering a greener tomorrow. We aspire to lead the solar energy revolution, driving innovation and accessibility to create a future where the sun's power is harnessed for the benefit of all.

Mission

Our mission at Dzuwagleam is to transform the way energy is produced and consumed.

Our Services

Solar Installation

Professional installation of solar panels, inverters, and related equipment. Ensuring compliance with local building codes and regulations.

Solar Monitoring Services

Installing monitoring systems to track the performance of solar installations. Providing analytics and reports on energy production and system efficiency.

Solar Consultation and Design

Assessing a client's energy needs. Designing customized solar solutions based on energy consumption and available space

Maintenance and Repairs

Routine maintenance to optimize system performance. Prompt troubleshooting and repair services for any technical issues

Energy Audits

Conducting energy audits to identify areas for energy efficiency improvements. Recommending and implementing energy-saving measures in addition to solar installations.

Financing and Leasing Options

Providing financing options to make solar installations more accessible. Offering leasing programs for customers who prefer not to purchase solar systems outright.
